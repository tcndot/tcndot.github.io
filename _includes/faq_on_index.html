Frequently Asked Questions

Where do you offer plot for sale services?

We offer plots for sale in the Delhi NCR region. Our current projects are Shyam Nagar, Shyam Kunj, and Shyam Vihar, located in Haryana near the Delhi border. Earlier, we also dealt in the Mathura–Vrindavan area; those projects are now completed.

Can we buy a plot on EMI?

Yes. We offer interest-free EMI options for up to 3 years. The exact EMI amount can be calculated using the calculator on our website, or you may visit our office for detailed guidance.

What benefits do we get if we buy on cash (one-shot payment)?

We offer 5-10% discount on one-shot payments. You get immediate possession of the property, and we also initiate registration in your name as soon as a court date is available. For full details, please visit our office.

Do you have a buyback policy?

Yes. We offer to buy back plots at the prevailing market rate. The booking amount is non-refundable. All original, company-signed receipts must be presented in physical form at our office.

What is your cancellation policy?

In case of cancellation, the amount received (excluding the booking amount) is refundable. The booking amount is non-refundable. All signed receipts must be presented in physical form at our office.

What payment modes do you accept?

We accept cash, UPI, cheque, and bank transfer. Cash payments must be made only at our physical office premises. Customers must collect a valid, company-signed receipt. The company will not be responsible for any loss or dispute arising from cash payments made outside the office or without a valid receipt.

How can I join your company as a real estate agent?

You can contact us through the Careers page on our website.

When will the documentation be done?

Documentation is done in stages. Once booking amount is paid, a file is prepared for future reference or remaining payments. After full payment, the property is registered or documentation for GPA is completed, as applicable.
